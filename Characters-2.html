Characters I've played

These are all the different people I've been over the course of my immersion into this game. My table tends to do a long running saga of campaigns wherein we play a bunch of different characters, sometimes even dipping into different systems.

Shadowrun Saga
Goblin (Shadowrun) Goblin was the first proper character I ever played in a TTRPG. He was a bit flat as I was still getting used to the idea of this type of gaming, and so I mostly just focused on one element of his backstory; the amount of debt he owed for his mech suit. All in all though, this was a really fun game and it got me hooked on this hobbey.
