Games I've run

These are all the different people I've been over the course of my immersion into this game. My table tends to do a long running saga of campaigns wherein we play a bunch of different characters, sometimes even dipping into different systems.
Dungeons and Dragons 5e
Curse of Strahd This was the first game I ever DMed. We went very far off the rails with this one.
Treski: A Tale of Two Dragons My first homebrew world. It involved two dragon gods and a prison on the moon, but we never got there.
Penumbra (One Shot) A modern magic one shot I ran for my fiance and some of her friends. It involved magical flowers and a strange new world.
A Magical Cape Town Adventure A continuation of Penumbra including a few more of my own friends.
The Wild Beyond the Witchlight Another prewritten module with the same group I did A Magical Cape Town Adventure with. We only ever got past the first hag though.
The Rise or Fall of the Nexus Municipality A whole new homebrew world featuring the gods created in the Rocktree saga.
Kids on Bikes
Hotel California (One shot) A one shot literally pulling from the lyrics of the song by The Eagles.
